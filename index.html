QuaLights

Light up your life with quality air.

According to the Environment Protection Agency, on average Americans spend 87% of their time indoors. That is a lot of time that people may be exposed to indoor air pollutants, yet most are unaware of the air quality of their living spaces. This is a big problem since the air we breathe in can have major effects on our health, especially for those with allergies triggered by air pollutants.

About QuaLights

Our goal is to educate people about the air quality of their living spaces while also helping them to take action in improving the air quality of their own home in order to foster a healthier living environment using QualLight.

Features

Powerful Features to Help You Improve Your Air Quality

Personalize

You can personalize which air irritants you want to track.

Monthly Report

Get monthly reports of air quality trends in your home.

Easy to Use Device

The QuaLights device is designed for you to be able to place anywhere in your home or hang on a ceiling or wall.

Hands Free Notifications

The light strip around the device will light up different colors depending on which air irritant it detected.

Learn

Learn about what you can do to improve your air quality.

Mobile Notifications

Get push notifications on your phone when there is a change in air quality or an emergency.
